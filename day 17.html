Overflow Examples

overflow: visible (default)

Content overflows this box and spills out visibly.

This line is very wide and overflows the box container area.

overflow: hidden

Content is clipped and hidden if it overflows.

This line is very wide and will be cut off.

overflow: scroll

Always shows scrollbars whether needed or not.

Scrollbars appear even if the content fits inside the box.

overflow: auto

Scrollbars only appear if the content overflows.

This will only show scrollbars if the content doesn't fit.
